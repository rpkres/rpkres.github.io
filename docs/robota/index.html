Robota

Robota is an intellectual property conceived and designed by Doug Chiang.
 

More

One of my roles as Development Director for the Console Games group at Sony Pictures Imageworks was locating prospective projects and I reached out to Doug about the possibility of making a Robota console game.

My responsibilities included discussions on games rights options, interfacing with project partners and game publishers.

I was heavily involved in the design and writing of concept documents and presentation material for projects.

Presentation Binder

 

More

Pictured above is one of a set of thermal-engraved, aluminum binders that I sourced for presentations.

The binders included the Executive Summary and Game Proposal documents, videos and concept art on the accompanying DVD.

I designed the package; co-authored and copy edited documents, edited the video and mastered the DVD.

A book of the same title was published. The author is Orson Scott Card with illustrations by Doug Chiang.
